About Us

Glaze Studio inspires curiosity and discovery around the art and craft of clay, drawing together students, artists, and an engaged public into a welcoming community.

And we are a welcoming family where people from all walks of life discover and nurture their creativity. We are artists and makers, teachers and students, innovators and visionaries.

Every day, in our classrooms, studios, galleries, and neighborhoods, we deepen the connection between people and clay.

Together, we are a force for good.
